Eve Sullivan

Introspection - 2022


Introspection is a photographic and glitch art series that serves as a conceptual self-portrait. The images take on a somewhat surreal linear narrative about the feelings of falling into a bout of anxiety and/or depression. As the images progress, they become more chaotic and the human features become less recognizable. The hope is that the more abstract or bizarre the images get the more that a sense of dissociation and dissonance is conveyed. The vivid and almost unnatural colors are meant to imply a quality that is similar to hallucinogenic, dreamlike, or fantasy to further emphasize the disconnection between a balanced and imbalanced mental state.
